> In the glittering snow beneath the alley streetlights,

> I clasped my hands together and made a wish.

> To think that beautiful snow would all melt away.

> To think they'd be the only trace left.

> But the sun rose high, and my heart melted away,

> Scattered by the wheels of passing bicycles.
